## Format

URIs from Science Live are of the form `https://w3id.org/sciencelive/np/RA…`. URIs from Nanodash (fallback) are of the form `https://w3id.org/np/RA…`. Both are valid and citable.

View any nanopub via the Science Live viewer by wrapping the URI:

```
https://platform.sciencelive4all.org/np/?uri=<full-URI>
```
